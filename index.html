Who is Lewis Capaldi?

Lewis Capaldi is a Scottish singer-songwriter known for his powerful, emotive voice and heartfelt ballads. He rose to fame with his breakout single “Someone You Loved” in 2018, which became a global hit. Despite his success, Capaldi is known for his down-to-earth personality, humor, and candidness about mental health, which have endeared him to fans worldwide.
